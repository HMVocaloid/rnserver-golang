Diamond Mine
Score Points by creating rows/columns of three or more gems.

Play Now!
Load Time: 33 seconds on 56K modem

Game Rules
- Swap any two adjacent gems by clicking on them in succession, so that rows or columns of three or more are formed. This will remove these gems from the board.
- Get extra bonus points for 'chain reactions' or 4+ gem rows.
- Fill bonus meter with gems for a special bonus points.
