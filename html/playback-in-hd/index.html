RealPlayer Features
Download For FREE
Download videos from thousands of sites with just one click!

Download video from thousands of sites, including YouTube, Facebook, TikTok – all in one click! Watch while you download. Subscribe to your preferred YouTube Channels for auto-downloads of your favorite content. Enjoy unlimited speed and quality up to 4K. On the go? Tag videos on your phone with RealPlayer Mobile, and they will be automatically added to your PC library.

Enjoy your videos on the big screen!

Play any video format. Bring your videos live to your TV with Chromecast. Cast and stream directly from your RealPlayer library and enjoy your content from your couch with big picture and sound. Not ready to cast? RealPlayer supports CD and DVD playback and burning.

Connect to RealPlayer Mobile and access your content from anywhere.

RealPlayer PC and Mobile are tightly integrated so you can take your media library on the road: access, stream and sync your media even when not connected to your home network-- your videos are never more than a click away. It’s child play to transfer videos taken on your phone to your PC, and you can keep your content safe and accessible with Cloud back-up.

Automatically find celebs and organize your video library by people

Instantly identify famous people in your videos and find fun facts. Save time and automatically create folders and tag videos by people you know. Browse, search, and discover people in all your videos. Simply opt-in to enable the feature at any time.

(US and Canada only)
Play and burn DVDs*

Create professional CDs and DVDs with built-in burning software. Burn your favorite playlists or videos with just the click of a button.

Simplify your life with all your media in one place.

Organize your media library all in one place so you can find what you want, when you need it. Access to all content in the new My Media tab. Create mixed playlists for videos and music with easy drag and drop. Declutter and automatically scan your library to remove duplicate videos*. Share your favorite media with friends and family.

Make your videos private*

Select videos you want to remain private in your library, so that only you can access them. It’s as easy as setting a PIN.

Convert videos

Convert videos to the most common video formats so you can watch all your videos with superior viewing quality. You can also turn video files into MP3 audio files to make them easy to listen to when on the go

Save space and remove duplicates

Declutter! Quickly and automatically identify duplicate videos or audio files in your library. RealPlayer recommends the best version to keep using a smart set ofa filters. More space helps your computer run smoother.

Enjoy many more features to enhance your experience

Easily trim videos to make them easier to share. Enjoy gorgeous album art for your Music collections. Automatically identify and label tracks for CD burning. Use the advanced equalizer to get the sound you want for playback or CD burning…

*Some features require a premium plan.
